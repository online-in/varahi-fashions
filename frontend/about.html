Our Story

Rooted in tradition, styled for today

Varahi Fashions began in Srikakulam with a simple idea: bring premium-quality pattu, fancy, soft silk, cotton and gift-collection sarees to every wardrobe — without the premium markup. We work directly with weavers and trusted mills so every saree carries genuine craftsmanship.

From WhatsApp order support to fast and safe home delivery, we designed the entire experience around how our customers already shop — simple, personal and dependable.
